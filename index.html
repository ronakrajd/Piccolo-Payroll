About Software

"Every thing in this world has its information"

P!ccolo Payroll is a web based software which can be used for calculating the salaries of employees. It keeps an record in its Attendence Management System. It provides an individual account to each employee. Employees can mark attendence from their own accounts and can see their attendence of the last one year. So there is no hassle for the management to manage the records of employees and calculating the salaries at the end of each and every month. Employees can apply for the leave online, the online requests are directly forwarded to the managing team of organization. It provides the statistics of the data about daily attendence, salary, salary graph of 10 years and also has a calender to display upcoming holidays. With its Mail System employees can mail others from their same account, with upcoming feature of attachments they will also be able to send the files on the go. No need to remember the credentials for the workplace account just use social login service and get connected.
